About the Game

Every Card Can Change the Outcome

Zhaimer is an original strategic memory card game created to challenge observation, planning and decision-making. Every player begins with hidden cards and must remember their values throughout the round.

Special cards introduce powerful abilities, including revealing hidden cards, exchanging positions and drawing additional cards. Players must decide when to take risks and when to protect their advantage.

The objective is simple: finish the round with the lowest possible score. Achieving it requires memory, timing and careful strategy.

Cards with Special Powers

Some cards do much more than add points. Learning when to use their abilities is a major part of mastering Zhaimer.

K♥

King

Gives the player a powerful card-drawing opportunity.

J♠

Jack

Allows cards to be exchanged, creating new strategic possibilities.

Q♦

Queen

Allows the player to reveal hidden information during the round.

10♣

Number Cards

Most number cards contribute their displayed value to the final score.

10♥

Zero-Point Cards

Certain special tens can reduce your total by counting as zero points.
