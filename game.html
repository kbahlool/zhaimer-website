About Zhaimer

Zhaimer is a strategic memory card game in which the goal is to finish with the lowest hidden-card total. Remember your cards, replace high values and use special powers at the right moment.

Getting Started

No download, install, or account is required. The game loads directly in your browser above — choose "Play vs AI" to start immediately, or create a room to invite friends once online multiplayer is available.

How to Play

Each player starts with four hidden cards and briefly views two. Draw, exchange or discard cards while protecting the information stored in your memory.

Card Values

Number cards usually equal their printed value. Picture cards usually count as 10. The 10 of Hearts and 10 of Diamonds count as zero.

Special Cards

The King provides a draw-two ability, the Jack allows a swap, and the Queen allows a one-time reveal.

Finish the Round

Declare finished when you believe your total is the lowest. After the final turns, all cards are revealed and the lowest score wins.

Game Modes

Play solo against AI opponents with adjustable difficulty, ideal for learning the rules. Online multiplayer with friends via private room codes is currently under development.

Supported Devices

Zhaimer runs in any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox — on desktop, tablet, and mobile. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size, and no app installation is needed.

Troubleshooting

If the game doesn't load, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Make sure JavaScript is enabled. If a problem persists, please report it here with your device and browser details.
