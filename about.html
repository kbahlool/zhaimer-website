About Zhaimer

Zhaimer is a strategic memory card game — hold the lowest hidden hand you can, using memory, calculated risk, and a handful of special powers to get there before your opponents do.

How to play

Full rules are on the game page under "How to Play."

Play your way

Play solo against AI opponents (1-5, three difficulty levels), or create a room and play online with friends in real time.
